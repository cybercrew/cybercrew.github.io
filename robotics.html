Robotics is the branch of technology that deals with the design, construction, operation, and application of robots. Robots are often used to perform tasks that are difficult for humans to perform or that are dangerous, such as exploring mines or defusing bombs. They are also used in manufacturing, transportation, and agriculture. There are many different types of robots, ranging from simple machines that can only perform a few specific tasks, to complex systems that can be programmed to do almost anything. Robotics technology is constantly evolving, and as it becomes more advanced, robots are becoming increasingly able to assist humans in a wider range of activities.
