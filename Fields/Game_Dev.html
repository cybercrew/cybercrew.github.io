2. Pre-Production

Game Design Document (GDD): Comprehensive document outlining the game.

3. Production

Development:

Roles: Lead Programmers, Gameplay Programmers, AI Programmers, Network Programmers

Art and Animation: Concept Artists, 3D Modelers, Animators, UI Designers

Audio: Sound Designers, Composers, Voice Actors

Design: Level Designers, Narrative Designers, Systems Designers

Outsourcing: External studios for specific assets or features.

Milestones: Alpha, Beta, Gold builds.

Testing and Quality Assurance: Continuous testing and bug fixing.

4. Post-Production

Polishing: Refining gameplay, visuals, and performance.

Roles: Developers across all disciplines.

Localization: Adapting the game for different languages and regions.

Roles: Localization experts, translators, voice actors.

Marketing and Promotion: Social media campaigns, influencer partnerships, press releases.

Roles: Marketing managers, PR specialists, community managers.

5. Release

Distribution: Launching on various platforms.

Roles: Distribution managers, platform liaisons.

Outsourcing: Partnering with distributors or publishers.

Launch Day Support: Monitoring and addressing issues immediately.

Roles: Support teams, live operations managers.

6. Post-Release

Updates and Patches: Regularly improving the game.

Roles: Ongoing support developers, community managers.

DLC and Expansions: Developing additional content.

Roles: Designers, programmers, artists.

Community Engagement: Building and maintaining a loyal player community.

Roles: Community managers, social media managers.

Evaluation: Analyzing the development process.

Roles: Project managers, team leads.

Archiving: Storing all project files and documentation.
