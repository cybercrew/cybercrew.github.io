The Crew

The CyberCrew is the IT Club of DPS Srinagar. It was founded in 2013, with an aim to provide a platform to the students of Kashmir to share and showcase their knowledge and skills. CyberCrew is famous for organising an annual event, TechKnow, which is the only IT related mega-event for schools in Jammu and Kashmir. We at the CyberCrew communicate using a language that only we decipher best. The CyberCrew provides an opportunity for all the IT fanatics of the school to take their first step towards realizing their dream of deciphering computers. The best part of it all is that we provide you with a beginning in learning IT at home i.e, in our own school.
